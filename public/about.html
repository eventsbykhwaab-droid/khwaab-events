About Khwaab

Decor rooted in feeling, culture, and care

Khwaab Events creates warm, elegant, desi-inspired event setups for meaningful celebrations across the GTA.

Our Approach

Thoughtful design for moments that matter

Khwaab was created with a simple belief: meaningful moments deserve thoughtful, intentional design. Rooted in Pakistani culture and refined through a modern lens, our work blends tradition with quiet luxury.

From nikkah ceremonies and mehndis to intimate gatherings and milestone celebrations, we focus on calm palettes, elegant textures, florals, and cohesive styling that feels warm, elevated, and personal.

What we care about

  • Design that fits your venue and guest flow
  • Soft, refined styling without feeling overdone
  • Clear communication before the event
  • Setup and takedown handled with care

Modern desi elegance

We create setups that feel culturally rooted while still looking clean, warm, and current.

Details with purpose

Every floral, candle, drape, and sign should support the mood of the celebration.

Built around your event

We adapt the decor direction based on your space, timing, budget, and priorities.
